v0.5.0 Latest Mar 9–10, 2026
  • Background replaced with a scrolling, blurred circuitry loop — GPU-accelerated via transform animation to prevent flickering, with grayscale filter applied
  • Text colors updated site-wide to remain readable against the dark background (body, headings, intro paragraphs, carousel section)
  • About Us profile cards filled in for Billy, Athena, and Maco — replacing the three placeholder cards
  • Athena's card features an animated regal purple-to-gold banner, floating petal burst on hover, and a soft glow pulse effect
  • Maco's card features an explosion shockwave with three expanding rings and a screen flash on hover
  • Profile avatars updated to support photo images from profileimages/
  • Changelog WIP overlay removed — page is now live
  • Changelog upcoming section added with collapsible "What to Expect" panel and pulsing purple dot
  • Quiz homepage — "Preview Modes" button added to WIP overlay; opens a tabbed modal with rules summaries for all five modes
  • Profile cards — 3D cursor-tracking tilt on hover with holographic light shimmer across all cards
  • Mark's card click animation overhauled — scene zooms out and blurs, cards dissolve in white light one by one, audio pauses for the video
  • Bug fixes — changelog timeline dot misalignment corrected; v0.5.0 entry missing wrapper div restored
  • Mark's card — shatter effect replaces the old crack animation; impact now spawns glass panel geometry radiating from center, with shards drifting apart in phase 2
  • Glass shard particles — post-shatter burst now uses angular polygon shards (triangles to pentagons) with a refracted white-to-ice gradient instead of circles; hover crystals on Mark's card reshaped to match
  • Shatter canvas isolated — effect is now confined strictly inside the card bounds (absolute positioning) rather than rendering over the full viewport
  • Video cue-in strictly enforced at 4.58 s — hard-seeks on reveal to correct any buffering drift
  • Post-video return — instant reset replaced with a spring zoom-in (cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.48, 0.64, 1)) and staggered element re-appearance, each fading in from a blurred desaturated state
  • White flash removed from the video outro; transition now fades the vignette out cleanly with no overexposure burst
  • Post-video atmosphere — fixed canvas spawns floating glass shards and god rays behind all UI; god rays are anchored to Mark's card and track its viewport position on scroll
