About this project

Our Mission

Web development is intimidating when you're just starting out. We made this so it doesn't have to be. Three modules. One quiz. Zero prior experience required.

3 Core Modules
5 Quiz Modes
1000+ Questions

🎯 Why We Built This

This website started as a school ICT project — but somewhere along the way it became something we actually wanted to make. Too many beginner resources assume you already know things, or bury the essentials under jargon. We wanted something that met people at zero, and walked them up from there.

📖 What We Cover

Three modules follow the exact curriculum we were taught: HTML for structure, CSS for styling, and JavaScript for behavior. Every concept is explained with examples, and where possible, with interactive demonstrations you can run right in the browser.

🎮 The Quiz Game

Knowing something and being able to use it under pressure are two different things. The quiz game was built to test the second one — five different modes, each designed to stress-test your knowledge in a different way. No multiple choice safety nets in some of them.

🏫 Our Scope

This project only covers what was taught to us during our school curriculum. We're not trying to replace W3Schools or MDN — we're trying to give you a starting point that's approachable, honest about what it doesn't cover, and built by someone who was in your position not that long ago.

✨ What We Hope You Absorb

That web development isn't magic. It's learnable. And that the best way to understand something is to build it yourself — even if it looks rough the first time. Especially if it looks rough.
