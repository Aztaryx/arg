Our Mission

Hello! Main developer here. I started this project because of a school project in our ICT subject. I told myself, wouldn't it be really, really funny if I made a website that teaches people how to, ironically, make a website? And not only that. I was planning to significantly deviate off course from what we learned. Before I started, I had NO clue on what the hell a meta charset is, nor font importing. I had to look at websites such as W3schools, which is the main inspiration for this website. I also removed approximately 1 or 2 hours from my already stupidly unhealthy sleep schedule of 6 hours to learn about coding. And what came out is this. I hope you enjoy, and I am PRAYING this becomes official, atleast in our school only.


Signing off, Aztaryx (Mark).
